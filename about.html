About my-startup

**Fluffyn** is a smart pet care platform designed to help pet owners manage every aspect of their pets' well-being in one place. The app allows users to create pet profiles, track vaccinations, schedule vet appointments, set medication and feeding reminders, and monitor health records. It also helps users discover nearby veterinarians, pet stores, grooming centers, and adoption services. Fluffyn includes features such as real-time activity tracking, appointment notifications, digital health records, and personalized pet care tips based on the pet's breed and age. With a clean, user-friendly interface, Fluffyn simplifies daily pet care while ensuring pets stay healthy, happy, and well cared for.
